⚑ Where Others Win (We Are Honest About It)

Speed — Semgrep Is 2.7× Faster

Semgrep is compiled OCaml with years of optimization. Our IFDS solver takes 87s vs Semgrep's 32s for 2,740 files. A Rust-native IFDS engine is on the v6.0 roadmap and will bring us under 10 seconds.

Language Coverage — Semgrep Has 30+

Ansede supports Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, Go, Java, and C#. Semgrep supports 30+ including Ruby, PHP, Kotlin, Swift, Rust, and more. New languages are being added based on user demand.

Rule Breadth — Different Philosophy

Ansede has fewer individual rules, but each one is AST-native and interprocedural. One IFDS rule catches patterns that would need 10+ regex-based rules in Semgrep. We optimize for signal, not rule count.

Enterprise Feature Maturity

SonarQube, Checkmarx, and Fortify have decades of enterprise polish, SSO, and compliance dashboards. Our Pro tier adds LLM triage and SBOM. Full enterprise portal with SSO is on the roadmap.
